Strategic Priorities

We help children by looking at more than a single issue. We treat the whole child. We examine the barriers and in many instances, these services are priorities in order to successfully integrate
into their home, school, and community environments.

Clothing

In the US, Americans throw away 18 million tons of textiles — about 85 percent of their clothes — each year, and the amount has doubled over the last 20 years. An average person throws away approximately 70 pounds of used clothing per year.
Clothes you donate have a bigger impact than you think. We are dedicated to alleviating clothing poverty and provide clothing support to thousands of children in South Florida as well as in Haiti.
