Become a Volunteer

Volunteers are the beating heart of SHCO.
Without them, we couldn’t help
the Children who Need Us.

Whether you can spare an hour or two, or want to get involved more often, whatever time you can give is invaluable.

Our incredible volunteers run shops, organise local fundraising branches, and put on cake sales and gala balls - join our amazing team and help build a better world.

We are a 501(c)3 nonprofit corporation, so any donations are tax-deductible (please see your financial advisor for details on charitable donations). SHCO does not discriminate against any child, regardless of religious, political or other affiliations.
